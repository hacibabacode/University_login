History

The foundation of Istanbul Aydin University and Anadolu Bil Vocational School is based on the Anatolian education and Culture Foundation.In 2007, Istanbul Aydin University completed its foundation and took its current place in higher education with 12 faculties, 2 colleges, 3 vocational schools and graduate education institute programs.

Vision

Istanbul Aydin University has adopted an understanding of excellence in education and training.His vision is to become a leading higher education institution where " individuals who will benefit humanity by turning knowledge into production are raised by acquiring professional competence in education programs that can renew themselves in line with contemporary changes, and sustainable innovations come to life.
